About Mila

Mila grew up in Fort Collins, Colorado, where she continues to live today. She was a self-taught artist in many mediums. Working at Xylem Design introduced her to woodworking. She now attends Savannah College of Art and Design in Atlanta, Georgia for graphic design.

About Woodburning

Woodburning is a technique in which images are burnt into wood using a hot rod.
